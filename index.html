About Me

My name is Tedd Hauser Jr. Currently I am working as an Operations Manager at a Target Distribution Center. Most of my careers has revolved around managing people, and making sure processes were being followed to make sure things were being done safely and efficiently.

Coding has always been an interest but there was no opportunity to pursue it. In high school, I enjoyed taking Visual Basic 1 & 2 but was not able to take classes beyond that. Target has recently started providing financial benefits to taking classes. After going through all the classes offered, Coding Bootcamp offered a class that did not focus on one type of coding, but many. Target also offered to cover this class 100% which was the last part of the puzzle in order to take this class.

As I get further into coding, I definitely understand the difficulty in learning something so vast in a short amount of time. Having a decent understanding of HTML, CSS, and JAVA, things are starting to fall together and make sense. As far as what I want to pursue further down the line, not 100% sure. As we learn about new coding applications, I will start to get a feel on the avenue I want to pursue and shift my learning in that direction. Whether I end up making websites or designing mobile applications, time will tell.
